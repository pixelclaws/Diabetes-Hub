Metformin / Biguanides

A type of Oral Hypoglycaemic Therapy

Properties: for weight stability but causes bloating and diarrhoe

Sulphonylureas

A type of Oral Hypoglycaemic Therapy

Properties: increases b-cell insulin secretion but causes hypoglycaemia and weight gain

Thiazolidinediones / Glitazones

A type of Oral Hypoglycaemic Therapy

Properties: increases insulin sensitivity but cause weight gain

Gliptins

A type of Oral Hypoglycaemic Therapy

Properties: a DPP4 inhibitor that stimulates stimulates insulin release

SGLT-2 inhibitors

A type of Oral Hypoglycaemic Therapy

Properties: lead to glucourea that lowers blood glucose overall

GLP-1 analogues

A type of Oral / Injected Hypoglycaemic Therapy

Properties: reduce major adverse CV events but cause GI problems

Gastric Bypass

A type of Bariatric Surgery

Properties: one of the safest operations in the entire field of surgery

Adjustable Silastic Gastric Banding

A type of Bariatric Surgery

Properties: ~50% of bands removed within 5 yrs due to technical problems

Sleeve Gastrectomy

A type of Bariatric Surgery

Properties: removes ~70% of the stomach from the body and is irreverible
